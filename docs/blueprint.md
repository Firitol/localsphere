# **App Name**: LocalSphere

## Core Features:

- AI-Powered Google Business Profile Content Tool: A generative AI tool that assists users in drafting compelling Google Business Profile descriptions, service lists, and promotional posts based on business-specific inputs, acting as a valuable content creation tool.
- Interactive Local SEO & GBP Readiness Checklist: A self-guided, interactive checklist and educational resource for businesses to identify areas for improving their local search presence and Google Business Profile optimization, offering actionable tips.
- Digital Service Inquiry Form: A clear and concise form allowing potential business clients to provide their contact information and specific needs for LocalSphere's digital services, facilitating lead generation.
- Local Business Profile Audit Tool (External View): A tool where users can enter their business name and location, which then displays publicly available information about their Google Business Profile, such as ratings, address, and primary categories via external Google Places API, without storing this data internally.
- Transparent Service Showcase & Pricing: Dedicated sections detailing the scope of LocalSphere's digital services (e.g., Google Business Profile optimization, local SEO, simple website development) with clear, static pricing models (one-time setup, monthly subscriptions).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#2E6AB0). This color evokes trust, reliability, and digital sophistication, anchoring the brand's identity as a leader in digital services. (Derived from H:220, S:60%, L:45%).
- Background color: A very light, cool grey-blue (#EEF3F8). This provides a clean, spacious, and inviting backdrop, enhancing content readability and offering a serene user experience. (Derived from H:220, S:20%, L:97%).
- Accent color: A vibrant aqua-cyan (#40C4E3). This refreshing hue is used to draw attention to interactive elements, calls-to-action, and key highlights, providing a dynamic contrast without overwhelming the primary palette. (Derived from H:190, S:75%, L:60%).
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif. Chosen for its exceptional legibility, clean lines, and neutral aesthetic, 'Inter' ensures content is easily consumable across all screen sizes and maintains a contemporary professional feel.
- Modern, clean, and cohesive line-art style icons should be used throughout the interface. Icons should be simplified abstractions that visually communicate complex concepts like 'optimization', 'analytics', or 'boost', reinforcing clarity and ease of understanding.
- Employ a spacious and responsive grid layout with generous white space to enhance readability and reduce visual clutter. Content hierarchy should be clearly defined through visual cues like font size, weight, and component separation, guiding users effortlessly through information.
- Implement subtle, purpose-driven micro-interactions for feedback and engagement. These include gentle fades on navigation changes, slight hover effects on interactive elements, and brief, smooth transitions for form submissions, creating a polished and intuitive user experience.
